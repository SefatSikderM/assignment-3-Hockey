Our Equipment

World class Equipment & Best Services ,Here are some items of hockey equipment: Stick: A long, thin, curved stick used to hit the puck or ball to score a goal. Helmet: A helmet with a face mask or visor. Gloves: Provide protection for your hands and a comfortable grip on your stick. Elbow pads: Protect your elbows from impacts with the ice, boards, or other players' sticks. Shin guards: Protect your frontal leg bones and knee caps. Hockey socks: Part of the team uniform and required to cover shin guards. Jersey: A hockey shirt with moisture management qualities. Hockey bag: Carries your skates, helmet, and pads, as well as other accessories
